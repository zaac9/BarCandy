Bar Candy's Mission

Our goal is to create a delightful experience and allow our customers to unleash their imagination through our sustainable products that can be enjoyed at a wide variety of events.

What is Bar Candy®?

Bar Candy® Edible Glasses are made from Belgian Chocolate without additives or preservatives. This patented item was developed by award winning mixologist Liz Green and was included in Martha Stewart TOP 5 BEST WEDDING TRENDS on Fox news, nominated twice Best New Product (Bizbash), and has garnered the attention of Colin Cowie, Bobby Flay and Kim Kardashian, to name a few.

Bar Candy® is packaged in quantities of 48 and can stand at room temperature for about 15 hours without melting, (out of direct heat), and refrigeration for up to 6 months and frozen for 12 months. Contact us for details if you would like to order a lesser quantity.

Bar Candy® can be enjoyed with Champagne, Specialty Cocktails, Mocktails, and Desserts. Flavors include, Dark, White, White with Swirls of any color, Dark with White Swirls. They can be rimmed with edible glitter, cookie bits, candy, sprinkles, etc. Use your imagination to create a Bar Candy® experience! Match colors, use garnishes, and create drinks and desserts to make it your own!

Creating a sustainable product was at the top of the priority list when we created Bar Candy®. The holders can be retained and used again, and the chocolate cups can be used for drinks without the holders and a customized sleeve is used. Glass holders are also available for restaurants and high end weddings. Kosher available.

About Bar Candy

Bar Candy creator Liz Green was a bartender in Los Angelos and started experimenting with flavors behind the bar. Before too long Liz was winning cocktail competitions and liquor companies were hiring her to create cocktails for their brands. She then created menus for restaurants, bars and nightclubs, and authored an original cocktail book called Cocktail Hollywood.

She ran a series of nightclubs in New York, and after realizing that the cocktail craze wasn’t going anywhere, she decided to create an experience. It was at this time that Bar Candy® was born!
